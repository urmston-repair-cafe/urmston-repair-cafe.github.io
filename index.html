Urmston Repair Café

What?

Repair Cafés are all about repairing things, with the goal to prevent throwing them away unnecessarily. At Urmston Repair Café we have a friendly team of volunteers who want to help fix your broken items. Bring them along to one of our events and we'll do our best to get them working again. We'll have a go at anything you bring in, but the things we specialise in are:

  • Electrical appliances
  • Computers
  • Toys
  • Tool and knife sharpening

You can learn more about the global network of Repair Cafés on their website. A list of other Repair Cafés around Manchester can be found here.

When?

We aim to run on the second Sunday of every month, from 9:30am until 11:30am. Please check our calendar to confirm an event is taking place before travelling.

Where?

Our events are hosted by local independent business Goodness Zero Waste. You can find them on Google Maps here. The address is:
